Who we are

A small initiative
with a clear mission

Clean World Project is a student-led environmental initiative dedicated to documenting existing sustainability technologies. Our mission is to educate the new and current generation to understand environmental challenges and promote responsible solutions that allow contribution to a cleaner and more sustainable world.


We are an early-stage initiative focused on documentation — not sales, not advertisement. We believe that access to clear, neutral information is the first step toward real environmental change.

What we do

Four pillars of our work

Education

Educating students and communities about environmental issues and how everyday actions can affect the planet.

Research & Documentation

CWP documents existing environmental technologies and solutions used around the world so organizations understand what already exists.

Community

We encourage the community to participate through programs, small projects and activities that allow people to engage with environmental topics.

Practical Solutions

CWP explores practical ways waste can be reduced, reused or managed using methods that already exist.

Our approach

Why Clean World
Project exists

We're a small, student-led, early-stage initiative created because existing environmental technologies are often hard to find, understand, or access. We focus on multiple aspects aside from documentation, such as outreach & collaboration with existing NGOs & Businesses. We currently have growing presence across Australia (Perth & Victoria), Singapore, the United States (California), Canada (Toronto) and Malaysia (Nationwide)

How we collect information
  • Official manufacturer websites & brochures
  • Public case studies & installations
  • News articles, academic or industry publications
  • Direct verification requests sent to manufacturers
We document, not advertise

Every technology we list is sourced from publicly available information or direct manufacturer contact. We do not accept payment for listings and carry no commercial bias.

Transparency by default

If manufacturer verification has not yet been received, it is clearly stated beside each listing. We won't present unconfirmed data as fact.

Built for everyone & Beyond documentation

Our research is available to governments, businesses, schools and individuals. We believe cleaner environments start with clearer information. While research and documentation are core to our work, we engage in outreach, collaboration and awareness to make environmental solutions accesible.
